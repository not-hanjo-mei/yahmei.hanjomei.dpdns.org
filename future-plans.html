Future Plans

What's next for YAH Mei? Exciting features on the horizon!

Upcoming Features

YAH Mei is constantly evolving. Here are some of the exciting features we're working on:

record_voice_over

Voice Recognition with FunASR

Coming Soon

We're integrating FunASR, an advanced speech recognition system, to allow YAH Mei to understand and respond to spoken commands and conversations. This will enable more natural interactions, especially in live streaming environments where voice is the primary communication method.

database

RAG Long-Term Support

Coming Soon

Retrieval-Augmented Generation (RAG) will give YAH Mei the ability to access, remember, and utilize specific information across conversations. This includes personal preferences, previous interactions, and knowledge databases, making conversations more contextual and personalized over time.

desktop_windows

GUI Support

Coming Soon

A user-friendly Graphical User Interface is being developed to make YAH Mei more accessible to users without technical backgrounds. The interface will provide easy setup, customization options, and real-time monitoring of YAH Mei's activities, all with a clean and intuitive design.
